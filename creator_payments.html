Creator Payments FAQ

When will I be paid?

Payments are processed within 30 days after the campaign deliverables have been reviewed and approved by the brand.

How will I be paid?

You will be paid via the payment method selected during onboarding, typically through PayPal or bank transfer.

What if the brand delays approval?

If the brand fails to respond within the specified timeframe, IFL will escalate the case and ensure payment is not unreasonably delayed.

Do I need to submit an invoice?

No. IFL will generate invoices on your behalf based on campaign agreements and approvals.

Will taxes be deducted?

Taxes are subject to your country’s regulations. IFL may be required to withhold taxes where applicable. You are responsible for complying with your local tax obligations.
